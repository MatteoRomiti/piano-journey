Piano Journey


An attemp to keep track of my little steps in learning to play the piano.


Timeline

Each musical composition appearing below ( in total) has a date representing when I started (more or less) learning it.



Progress

The stars represent how good I can or could play a piece

  • 5 Stars: I played it at a recital
  • 4 Stars: I can play it quite fluently
  • 3 Stars: I can play it but not fluently
  • 2 Stars: I can play more than half but not fluently
  • 1 Star: I can play less than half and not fluently
